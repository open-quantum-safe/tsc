# Open Quantum Safe Health Reports

Health reports for the Open Quantum Safe organization will be generated on a quarterly basis. They will be employed to evaluate the overall 
status of the project. These updates will review progress against established goals, analyze developments over the reporting period, 
and evaluate the impact of any measures implemented on the project’s health, as defined by a set of metrics. 
Based on this assessment, the report will also refine and update the corresponding recommendations, ensuring that proposed actions 
remain aligned with current conditions and support continuous improvement.

These reports will be generated with the help of AI.
